Pixar - 1989

Pixar was founded in 1986. Pixar is a computer animation Studio that was originally named Special Effects Computer Group at Lucasfilm which was launched in 1979. Pixar makes films and segments that are computer animated. In 1986, Steve Jobs, the co-founder of Apple bought Pixar for 5 million and then invested another 5 million into the company. Pixar became a very successful company that had many Oscar-winning films. In 1995, Disney distributes Toy Story which becomes the first feature length computer animated film. In 2004, Disney and Pixar could not agree on a new agreement so they on looking for new partners. Pixar was later bought by Disney in 2006 for 7.6 billion. Disney Pixar still makes many different animated films today that are very popular.

Microsoft Office - 2013

Office 365 had a stable release in 2013. This is when they had an updated version of Office 365. Microsoft office was a software product that was subscription based. They had monthly and annual packages of Microsoft programs such as Microsoft Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, Publisher, and many more programs.These subscriptions also came with 1 TB of cloud storage on One Drive. There were multiple subscription plan that were available. For example, home, personal, university, business, and enterprise.
